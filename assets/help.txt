Introduction

You are a Gravity Hunter, a space police.
Shoot down enemies. Planets bend your shots.
Use gravity to score big with long flight paths.


Controls

Aim (coarse): Left stick (ship faces stick direction)
Aim (fine): D-Pad Left/Right
Shot Power: D-Pad Up/Down
Modifiers: Hold L1 for smaller, R1 for larger steps
Fire: x
Thrust: o


HUD

Red bar: Health
Green bar: Shot power
Yellow bar: Energy (auto-regenerates)
You need at least 50% energy to start thrusting.


Gravity & Scoring

Planets curve projectiles; faster shots curve less.
Each hit grants points.
A longer flight time scores more points.
Shots can travel slightly beyond the screen.


Tactics

Reposition to take cover or line up better angles.
Blend quick, straight shots with slow,
curving slingshots for high scores.
Use thrust to dodge enemy fire or adjust your aim.
